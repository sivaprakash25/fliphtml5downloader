How to download a FlipHTML5 book as PDF

This free FlipHTML5 PDF converter lets you save flipbooks from online.fliphtml5.com when no download button is available on the viewer. Copy the book URL from your browser, paste it above, and click Download PDF.

What this tool does

  • Reads the online config.js from the FlipHTML5 viewer
  • Downloads all page images in full quality
  • Merges pages into one portable PDF file
  • Shows live progress while your book is processed

Supported links

Any public FlipHTML5 viewer URL, including links with page numbers like #p=1, #p=2, and short links from fliphtml5.com.
